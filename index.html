About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Statistics.
My research interests are mathematical statistics, Bayesian inference, and statistical decision theory. I study both spherically symmetric distributions and non-normal models such as multivariate skew-normal distributions. I also find minimax shrinkage and prediction problems fascinating.
